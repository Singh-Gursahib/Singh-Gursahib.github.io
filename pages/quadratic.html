Find Roots of quadratic equation:

Quadratic Quest: The Ultimate Root-Finding Adventure

Instructions:

The quadratic equation must be of the form: ax² + bx + c = 0

Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. 1. Enter the coefficients a, b, and c for your equation in the respective input fields.
  2. 2. Click the Calculate button to find the roots of the equation.
  3. 3. The roots will be displayed below in the "Roots of the equation" section.
  4. > If you wish to clear the input values and results, click the Clear button.

--Please note that the roots can be real and distinct, real and equal, or complex numbers depending on the discriminant value.
